A Quick Rundown on Brewery Types:

  • micro - Most craft breweries.

  • nano - A small brewery that usually only distributes locally.

  • regional - A regional location of an expanded brewery.

  • brewpub - A restauraunt/bar with a brewery on-site.

  • large - A very large brewery typically not for visitors.

  • planning - A brewery in it's planning stages or just not yet open.

  • bar - Simply, a bar. No brewery on-site.

  • contract - A brewery using another brewery's equipment.

  • proprietor - Similar to a contract brewery but more of a brewery incubator.

  • closed - You guessed it, it's closed.
